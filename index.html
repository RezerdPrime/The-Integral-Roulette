The Integral List

They are grouped into 3 levels of difficulty:

1) Beginner - Here are integrals that require the ability to apply the most basic methods, such as:

- substituting a variable,

- integrating by parts,

- constructing a recurrence formula;

2) Advanced - Integrals of this group require knowledge of a second course in mathematical analysis.

Here you will need to:

- be able to use differentiation over a parameter,

- integrate under the sign of the integral,

- know some things about series and a few special functions;

3) Master - Here are brutal integrals that require a deeper knowledge of special functions, calculus,

and even complex analysis. In addition, very specialized formulas may be required.
